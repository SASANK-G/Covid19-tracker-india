Symptoms

Spreading of COVID

Coughing Or Sneezing

Coronavirus can spread if an infected person coughs or sneezes

Human Contacts

Direct skin-to-skin contact increases likelihood of infection.

Contained Objects

Unsanitized or infected surfaces can increase the likelihood of infection

Preventions

Always take these precautions to protect yourself and loved ones from the novel coronavirus.

Wash your hands as frequently as you can, especially before meals, after coughing or sneezing

Always wear a mask in public places. Dicard or wash the mask after usage.

Avoid close contact with people who are unwell. Maintain 3 feet distance atleast in public places

Stay home and self-isolate from others in the household if you feel unwell
